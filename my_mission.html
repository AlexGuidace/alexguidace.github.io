My Mission

Meaningful Code: A Personal Definition

I see software engineering as many things.

In one sense, I see its result as utilitarian: software is a tool that helps an individual person, business, or organization accomplish tasks; the bottom line--it's useful and practical across a variety of domains.

In another sense, it's an arcane, almost magical skillset--a complex weaving of hieroglyphics and many small puzzles that integrate together to form a cohesive, functioning system or ecosystem. It's beautiful, in its own way.

In a third, near-to-one's-heart and inspired sense, I see software as a vehicle, launching humanity towards a better future, one where the negative aspects of the human condition are minimized through the use of science and technology.

I am motivated by each of the aforementioned points, but it is this last point that really excites and drives me to do this type of work. Software enables better living standards for everyone now, and into the future; this feels meaningful to me. I believe software does and will continue to play an integral part in the shaping of humanity towards a positive future.

Meaningful Web Development

Web development is my field of choice as a software engineer. Web applications—whose foundation, the internet, ushered us into the Information Age—play a vital role in bringing good into this world—in driving humanity forward. As discussed in the last section, I am driven to contribute to these types of personally meaningful applications. Below are some social impact and tech-for-good industries that I'd be excited to wake up every day and develop web applications for:

  • Medicine research and clinical applications
  • Science and engineering research applications
  • Space tech applications
  • Civic/government improvement applications
  • Virtual reality applications
  • Augmented reality applications
  • Criminal justice applications
  • Education applications
  • Agriculture applications
  • Environmental sustainability applications
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning applications
