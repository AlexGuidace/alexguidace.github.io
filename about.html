About Alex.

I unapologetically love computers, and by extension, love being a programmer.

Computers and their software have enabled humankind to do things once thought magical—or never even imaginable—to past generations: the internet, advanced space flight, artificial intelligence, insane medical technologies that save lives, simulations of our universe, self-driving vehicles, streaming entertainment services, video games, and yes, even Roombas.

I could go on and on, and even about things far less flashy than the aforementioned, such as productivity tools like Jira, but I’ll stop here.

To sum it up, computer technologies have made the lives of us mere mortals easier and far more enjoyable; they all solve human problems. And the driving force behind these technologies? Programmers.

I love programming because it creates solutions for a plethora of society’s problems. Programming gives me the chance to make a difference and perhaps help alleviate the pressures of the human condition in my own small way.

So what qualifies me to solve these problems?

Here’s the evidence:

  • Worked at one of the world’s leading science and problem-solving institutions, NASA, as a programmer and researcher.
  • Have had a number of roles in IT, tech writing, and UX design.
  • Have designed and built significant computer programs.
  • Have completed several massive open online computer science and programming courses.
  • Have experience across a wide swath of technology stacks. This means I learn fast.

The bottom line? I have an affinity for finding solutions.

And that affinity has manifested itself ultimately in the creation of computer programs.

And of course, it is ALL interesting to me… but one needs to specialize.

That said, I probably think the internet as a whole is the coolest, most transformative technology ever conceived, so my focus lies there.

I have experience building full stack applications, so I can do both frontend and backend work. Given my natural appreciation for user experience and beautiful designs, I love the frontend side of things, but also tend to enjoy the mental abstraction involved in backend systems and logic. Really, as long as I’m solving a problem, I’m going to enjoy it.

I’m open to new opportunities, so please reach out if our visions align and you want a creative, responsible person on your team!
